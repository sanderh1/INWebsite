The history of consoles

The first video game consoles were created in the 70's, with consoles such as the
Magnavox Oddysey and Coleco Telstar. These were all part of the first generation
of consoles. These consoles served as an inspiration for the first succesful arcade
game, Pong.

The Magnavox Oddysey


As technology became more advanced, so did consoles. The first popular consoles
only began appearing in the third generation, which lasted from 1983 - 2003. These
include the Nintendo Entertainment System, Sega Master System and the Atari 7800.

The Nintendo Entertainment System

Where are we at now?

Now we've entered a futuristic area, with consoles such as the PS4, Xbox One and the
announcing of the long awaited PS5 and Xbox Series X/S.


Which companies are popular?

Microsoft

Microsoft is one of the leading companies in terms of consoles, being the creator of the
Xbox and Kinect game consoles. It is also number 3 on the global top 10 gaming companies,
and is very well known for their computer software.

Sony

Sony is probably at the top of its game, being the creator of the very popular Playstation series.
Next to that, they've also created a few lesser known consoles like the Vita. They're also the
biggest gaming company in the world!
