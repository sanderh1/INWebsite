Brands


1. Sony

Sony stands at number 1 having earned a gaming revenue of $20.3 billion.
It was founded in 1946 and It’s headquarters is now stationed in Tokyo, Japan.
The most profitable item they have sold is the PlayStation 4 back in 2019,
when they earned the most off of it.


2. Nintendo

At number 2 comes Nintendo, founded in 1889 It's an extremely old gaming company.
It has earned a total revenue of $11.1 billion. Their most profitable product was the
Nintendo Switch. It's mainly known for their most influential and famous gaming character, Mario.


3. Microsoft

In 3rd place comes Microsoft with It's Xbox One. It's mainly known for creating computer software,
but has also created a foothold in the gaming industry. It currently stands at a total revenue of
$11.0 billion, and was founded in 1975 by Bill Gates.


4. Tencent

In 4th place comes a company whose name Isn't very well known in the gaming community, but certainly has (had)
a massive influence. It has a total revenue of $9.2 billion, and was founded in 1998. It's headquarters are stationed
in Shenzhen, China. It's biggest game is League of Legends, which is very well known.


5. Activision Blizzard

At last place we have Activision Blizzard. It is mainly known for Call of Duty, with It’s most profitable Call of Duty being Call of Duty: Modern Warfare.
It was founded in 2008 and is stationed in Santa Monica, USA. It's total gaming revenue is $6.5 billion, making it the fifth largest gaming company.
